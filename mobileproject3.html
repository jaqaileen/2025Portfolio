Bumi (1/2)

Brand Identity | Web Design

Based in Jakarta, BUMI Museum is a Natural History and Geology museum dedicated to bridging the worlds of science, art, and cultural heritage. It offers visitors an immersive understanding of Indonesia’s rich geological history while nurturing a sense of wonder and responsibility toward preserving the earth’s treasures.

I created the brand identity guide book for BUMI Museum, developing a visual and narrative system that reflects its mission to connect people with the natural beauty and history of our planet.

Size: 8.26 x 6 in
Binding: Spiral
Typeface: Ghost, Helvetica Neue
