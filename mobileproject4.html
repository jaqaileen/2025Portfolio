Bumi (2/2)

Brand Identity | Web Design

Located in Jakarta, BUMI Museum explores Indonesia’s geological past through a blend of scientific discovery and artistic storytelling. Its mission is to inspire appreciation and care for the earth’s natural heritage.

Building on the museum’s visual language, I designed and developed a website that showcases BUMI’s collections and values through an interactive and cohesive digital experience based on the brand identity I created.
